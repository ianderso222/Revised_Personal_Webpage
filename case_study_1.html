The goal of this website was simple; to act as a showcase for work in a graphic design portfolio.

Through discussions with the client, we decided a minimal page would be the best choice for this type of website. A minimal website would allow the work to stand out against a simple background, allowing the viewer to focus on her work and not be distracted. The white background and use of negative space reminds us of an art exhibition or museum, drawing the eye to the individual work. A grid based gallery of thumbnails allows viewers a quick glimpse at the overall style, and expanding images allow a closer look into each individual piece.

With this website the client has done freelance design work for individuals and businesses all over the world and has recently secured a position as a graphic designer for a professional sports team.
