Full disclosure: I completed the "Taco Lobo" site in about a day. I designed it in the middle of redoing my personal webpage just as a quick refresher after staring and tweaking the same site multiple times over.

I wanted this website to reflect everything I desired to see when visiting a restaurant's website. I want to see is a menu on the webpage, not a downloadable pdf that opens a new window taking me away from their website. I want to see some pictures and get a sense of the style of restaurant I am viewing. Is it formal? Casual? Steakhouse? Italian? These things should be apparent as soon as the page loads. Also, I want the address and hours in an easily accessible location.
